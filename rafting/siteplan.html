Site Name

Get Wet-The Best Rapids in Idaho!

Site Purpose

The purpose of the website: I want my site to hit people with the idea of cutting loose and having fun.
I want it to be colorful and have awesome images of people having fun on the rapids.
I want there to be easy to read links that show different activities that are available.
A link that takes you to a cost breakdown of daytrips or muti-day adventures and scheduling availability. And a link to merchandising.

Target Audience and Scenarios

My target audience will be family vacations, youth adventures and corporate team building activities.
First and foremost, this will be a desktop site, but I want a simple mobile version as well.
